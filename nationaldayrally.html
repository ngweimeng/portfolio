Leadership in Words: Analyzing PM Lee's National Day Rally Speeches

Using NLP to Uncover Leadership Themes and Sentiments in PM Lee's Speeches

Skills: Python, NLP, BeautifulSoup, OpenAI GPT, TextBlob

As Singapore's Prime Minister, PM Lee Hsien Loong has delivered numerous National Day Rally speeches, reflecting the nation's values, progress, and leadership challenges. This project was initiated in light of PM Lee's impending leadership transition, aiming to analyze the themes and sentiments in his speeches over time using Natural Language Processing (NLP).

Our analysis spans from 2004 to 2023, using a collection of speeches sourced from the Prime Minister's Office website. The project uncovers trends in leadership themes and sentiments, contextualizing the social and economic messages conveyed by PM Lee. This analysis is for academic purposes only, without political implications or endorsements.

We conducted a series of analyses, including:

  • Text Summarization: Summarizing speeches using OpenAI’s GPT model to highlight key themes and conclusions.
  • Sentiment Analysis: Assessing the sentiment polarity and subjectivity with TextBlob to capture emotional tone over the years.
  • Topic Modeling: Employing Non-negative Matrix Factorization (NMF) to extract and track topics within the speeches.
  • Word Clouds: Visualizing frequent terms across speeches by year, revealing topical focus.
  • TF-IDF Analysis: Quantifying the importance of words within the corpus to highlight unique content in individual speeches.

Data Collection: Speeches were gathered using Python's `requests` and `BeautifulSoup` libraries, enabling a streamlined, systematic approach to data scraping.

Data Preprocessing: Text data was cleaned by removing stopwords, special characters, and numbers, and applying lemmatization for effective NLP processing.

The analysis revealed evolving themes in leadership, societal progress, and challenges, offering insight into the overarching narrative of PM Lee’s speeches. While certain trends were expected, such as emphasis on unity and resilience, other findings—like shifts in emotional tone and specific focal topics—provided a nuanced perspective on his leadership over time.

“Leadership is about making others better as a result of your presence and making sure that impact lasts in your absence.” ~ Sheryl Sandberg

This project highlights the potential of NLP in political speech analysis, with PM Lee’s speeches providing a rich dataset for observing shifts in thematic and emotional content. By applying these techniques, we hope to spark deeper understanding and dialogue around the impact of leadership narratives.

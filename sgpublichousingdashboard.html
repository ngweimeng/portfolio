Singapore Public Housing Sales Dashboard

Analyzing resale prices of Singapore Public Housing using Tableau Dashboard

Skills: Tableau

For young Singaporean adults looking to start a family, HDB flats (Singapore Public Housing) would be their top choice, especially when budget constraints are in play. Yet, navigating the myriad of choices - from choosing the optimal location, idea size, down to the variety of flat types - can be overwhelming. Recognising this challenge, I've created a dashboard to better visualise how certain factors impact public housing prices in Singapore using Tableau.

The housing price dataset can be retrieved from data.gov.sg.

Area of improvement: Perform feature engineering to pre-process columns such as premium on proximity to town, premiums in flat model and distance to amenities such as train stations or shopping malls.
